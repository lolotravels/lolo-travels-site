Upload your invoice and submit for Lolo approval. Once approved, Lolo generates the government invoice automatically.

When you submit a payment request, Lolo reviews and approves it. Upon approval, the system automatically generates an IPP-format invoice from Lolo Travels LLC to the contracting agency using your deliverable details and Lolo credentials (EIN 41-4888165, CAGE 1WR24). You receive payment to your bank account on file.
