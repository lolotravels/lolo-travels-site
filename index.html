"The most transformative journeys are the ones that feel personal. Lolo Travels was built so every client feels that."

Lolo Travels was founded on a belief that a well-designed journey changes how you see the world — and yourself. Founded with deep multicultural roots — Guyanese, Grenadian, Trinidadian, and Ethiopian — woven into every experience we design.

Years of extensive travel across six continents, including extended time in Uruguay and throughout the Caribbean, shaped the approach that defines Lolo Travels: deeply personal, culturally informed, and operationally precise.

Today, Lolo Travels is a woman-owned travel management and logistics firm providing global access, risk-integrated planning, and fully managed experiences for individuals, businesses, and organizations.

Travel Protection

Protected at
Every Latitude

Built In. Not Bolted On.

Protection is part
of every trip we plan.

As a licensed insurance agent, we integrate travel protection from the beginning — not as a checkbox at checkout. Every trip we manage includes a protection review and coverage options tailored to your specific journey.

Trip Cancellation & Interruption

Full protection against unforeseen cancellations, delays, and disruptions — before and during your journey.

Emergency Medical & Evacuation

Priority medical coverage and emergency evacuation services — domestic and international — when you need it most.

Baggage & Delay Coverage

Protection for lost, damaged, or delayed luggage and high-value personal assets throughout your travel.

Cancel For Any Reason (CFAR)

Maximum flexibility — recover a significant portion of your investment for any cancellation reason, where available.

Supplier Default Protection

Coverage if an airline, hotel, or tour operator goes out of business before or during your trip.

24/7 Crisis Assistance

Round-the-clock access to our assistance network for medical, logistical, or emergency support anywhere in the world.

Important Information

Travel protection is offered in connection with the travel services provided by Lolo Travels LLC. Coverage is underwritten by licensed insurance carriers and subject to the terms, conditions, and exclusions of the applicable policy. Not all coverages are available in all jurisdictions.

You have the right to decline travel protection. If you choose to decline, please note that you will be responsible for costs associated with trip cancellation, interruption, or other covered events. A record of your acceptance or declination will be maintained on file.
