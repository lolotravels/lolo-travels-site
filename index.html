The Founder

A Journey Born
From Within

"The most transformative journeys are the ones that feel personal. Lolo Travels was built so every client feels that."

Lolo Travels was founded on a belief that a well-designed journey changes how you see the world — and yourself. Founded by a California native with roots in Minnesota, our agency carries a multicultural heritage — Guyanese, Grenadian, Trinidadian, and Ethiopian — woven into every experience we design.

Years of extensive travel across six continents, including extended time in Uruguay and throughout the Caribbean, shaped the approach that defines Lolo Travels: deeply personal, culturally informed, and operationally precise.

Today, Lolo Travels is a woman-owned travel management and logistics firm — SAM.gov registered and licensed in travel and insurance — providing global access, risk-integrated planning, and fully managed experiences for individuals, businesses, and organizations.
