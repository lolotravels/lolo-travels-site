The Founder

A Journey Born
From Within

"The most transformative journeys are the ones that feel personal. Lolo Travels was built so every client feels that."

Lolo Travels was founded on a belief that a well-designed journey changes how you see the world — and yourself. With deep multicultural roots — Guyanese, Grenadian, Trinidadian, and Ethiopian — every experience we design carries genuine cultural intelligence.

Years of travel across six continents shaped our approach: deeply personal, culturally informed, and operationally precise. We built this firm because the world deserves to be experienced fully — not just visited.

Today, Lolo Travels is a woman-owned travel management and logistics firm providing global access, risk-integrated planning, and fully managed experiences for individuals, businesses, and organizations.

Travel Protection

Protected at
Every Latitude

Built In. Not Bolted On.

Protection is part
of every trip we plan.

We integrate travel protection from the beginning. Every trip includes a protection review and coverage options tailored to your specific journey.

Trip Cancellation & Interruption

Full protection against unforeseen cancellations, delays, and disruptions — before and during your journey.

Emergency Medical & Evacuation

Priority medical coverage and emergency evacuation services — domestic and international.

Baggage & Delay Coverage

Protection for lost, damaged, or delayed luggage and high-value personal assets.

Cancel For Any Reason (CFAR)

Maximum flexibility — recover a significant portion of your investment for any cancellation, where available.

Supplier Default Protection

Coverage if an airline, hotel, or tour operator ceases operations before or during your trip.

24/7 Crisis Assistance

Round-the-clock access to our assistance network for medical, logistical, or emergency support anywhere.

Important Disclosure

Travel protection is offered in connection with travel services provided by Lolo Travels LLC. Coverage is underwritten by licensed insurance carriers and subject to the terms, conditions, and exclusions of the applicable policy. Not all coverages are available in all jurisdictions.

You have the right to decline travel protection. If you choose to decline, you will be responsible for costs associated with cancellation, interruption, or other covered events. A record of your acceptance or declination is maintained on file.
