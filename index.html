The Founder

A Journey Born
From Within

"The most transformative journeys are the ones that feel personal. Lolo Travels was built so every traveler feels that."

Lolo Travels was born from a simple truth: the most transformative journeys are the ones that feel personal. Founded by a California native who found new roots in Minnesota, our agency carries a rich multicultural heritage — Guyanese, Grenadian, Trinidadian, and Ethiopian — woven into everything we create.

Having lived and traveled extensively — including years in Uruguay and throughout the Caribbean — our founder built Lolo Travels not as a business, but as a vocation. A calling to help others experience the world with the same depth, intention, and joy discovered through a life of exploration.

Today, Lolo Travels is a proudly woman-owned agency crafting bespoke journeys that honor culture, celebrate beauty, and deliver unmatched personal service to those who expect the extraordinary.
