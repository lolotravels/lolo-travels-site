Your coverage tier

Shield Plus

Everything in Plus, plus discreet security detail coordination, elevated-risk destination protocol, priority 24-hour response team, and family office coordination for complex multi-party travel.

Before you go
Destination intelligence brief
Entry requirements, current safety conditions, cultural notes, currency, and what to expect on arrival — from on-the-ground contacts, not a guidebook. Delivered 72 hours before departure.
Cyber security briefing
Unsecured Wi-Fi risks, device security abroad, charging station hazards, and digital exposure at your destination. Automatic on every international trip.
Passport and visa tracking
All passports on file. Entry requirements checked per destination. Flagged nine months before any expiry.
While you are there
Local contact in your phone
A vetted local contact saved to your phone before you land — one call away for anything, any hour.
24-hour Lolo line
Lolo is always reachable. A disruption, a property issue, a medical question, an emergency — one message and someone responds. Always.
Real-time itinerary monitoring
Lolo watches your flights. Delays and rebookings are handled before you land confused. Your downstream reservations adjust automatically.
After you return
Claims recovery
If something went wrong — a missed connection, a property that didn't deliver, a disrupted tour — Lolo manages the claim from submission through resolution. No recovery, no fee.
