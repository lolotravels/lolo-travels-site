How It Works

Three steps. You do one of them.

We take on the work of pursuing your claim so you do not have to spend hours on hold with airlines, hotels, or insurance companies.

01
Submit Your Claim
Fill out this form. Tell us what happened, who owes you, and how much. Upload any documents you have. Takes 10 minutes.
02
Lolo Takes Over
Our team reviews your case, contacts the responsible party, and manages all correspondence and escalations on your behalf.
03
You Get Paid
When your claim is resolved, you receive the recovered amount minus Lolo's recovery fee. No recovery, no fee.
What We Recover

We handle every type of travel claim.

If a carrier, hotel, or insurer owes you money, Lolo pursues it.

F
Flight Disruptions
Cancellations, lengthy delays, missed connections, denied boarding, tarmac delays, and rebooking expenses.
H
Hotel and Accommodation
Overbooking, early departure fees, unauthorized charges, service failures, and uninhabitable room conditions.
B
Baggage Claims
Lost luggage, delayed baggage, damaged items, and out-of-pocket expenses during baggage delays.
I
Travel Insurance
Denied or underpaid insurance claims including trip cancellation, medical evacuation, and emergency care.
C
Cruise Line Disputes
Itinerary changes, missed ports, service failures, medical incidents, and shore excursion cancellations.
T
Tour and Package Claims
Tour operator failures, package components not delivered, last-minute changes, and operator insolvency.

Recovery Terms
Lolo operates on a contingency basis - you pay only when we recover.
Flat-fee option: For straightforward claims under $1,000, Lolo charges a flat intake fee of $75, regardless of outcome.

Percentage option: For all other claims, Lolo charges 25% of the amount recovered. If we recover nothing, you owe nothing.
